Services

WePlay is an app to meet new people, to make friends, to make memories, and to be human again in the post-Facebook era. We believe in bringing people together irl.

Be in the know

WePlay allows you to view events going on near you using real-time, location based information, displayed beautifully on a map

Human in Mind

Human interaction should be human. WePlay helps foster human interaction through events and parties that require physical attendance.

Millenial Friendly

WePlay was created with our generation in mind. A social media app which helps you meet people in real life. Can life get any better?

Design

  • Where ya at?
  • Just Swipe Left
  • Step in the Right Direction

View all that's going on in your area. All you have to do is turn on your location and the map will populate with events, whether it's parties, concerts, or your local book club meeting.

Want to know where people in your network are going? Swipe left away from the map and see what events your friends are attending.

If you want to view all of your events simply swipe right away from the map and see all of the events listed out. You can also filter through the events.
